My Projects
Pre-term infant diet calculator
Diets (i.e. caloric & micronutrient intake) is everything in NICU! Pre-term infant diets are calculated based on the baby's weight and anemic status.

Before my project:

These calculations were done manually which made them very time-consuming and error-prone.

It was one dietitian's full-time job to do all these calculations.

The incorrect calculations led to suboptimal nutrition for our babies!

So I created a pre-term infant diet calculator in Excel. I included a macro button to screenshot the results so it can be seamlessly pasted into the patient's chart.

Results of my project:

My calculator saved the unit 40 hours of work a week (or $50,000 annually).

Our pre-term infants were getting the proper nutrition they needed which led to better clinical outcomes!
